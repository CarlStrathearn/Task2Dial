R286 Mashed-Potato Stuffed Chicken Breast https://cookeatshare.com/recipes/mashed-potato-stuffed-chicken-breast-705464

This recipe takes 45 minutes to cook, serves 4 and contains 9 ingredients,  [''baking potatoes'', ''milk'', '’butter'', '’lemon'', ''salt'', ''black pepper'’, ''chicken breast'', ''olive oil'', ''fresh parsley'']

User: I would like to cook a mashed-potato stuffed chicken breast, what are the ingredients required to make this meal?
Robot: You will need 2 medium peeled and cubed baking potatoes, ¼ cup of milk, 4 tbsp of butter, 1 medium lemon that is zested, ¼ tsp of salt, ⅛ tsp of ground black pepper, 4 chicken breast halves, 1 tbsp of extra-virgin olive oil, 2 tbsp of fresh parsley that is chopped.
User: I have the ingredients ready, what is the first step that I need to take?
Robot: Preheat your oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit, then in a medium saucepan, bring the potatoes and enough water to cover and bring to a boil.
User: I have preheated the oven to 400 degrees and brought the potatoes and added enough water to cover and brought it to a boil, what do I do next?
Robot: Now lower the heat and simmer until the potatoes are fork-tender for approximately 15 minutes, then drain them and return the potatoes to the pot.
User: I have lowered the heat and simmered for the amount of time required, I also drained them and returned the potatoes to the pot. What do I have to do now?
Robot: Now, add ¼ cup of milk, 2 tbsp of butter and 1 lemon peel and mush until they are all smooth.
User: I have added the milk, butter and lemon, they are now smooth, what do I do next?
Robot: Good, now season with salt and pepper, then transfer the potatoes to a work surface and flatten them into an even round and cut them into quarters.
User: I have seasoned with salt and pepper and transferred the potatoes to a work surface and flattened them and cut them into quarters. What should I do next?
Robot: Next, run your fingers underneath the skin of each chicken breast to separate it apart from the meat.
User: I have separated the breast apart from the meat, what do I do now?
Robot: Now, place the stuffed chicken inside a roasting pan, drizzle with 1 tbsp of olive oil and season with salt and pepper, then bake until they go tender and the juices run clear for approximately 40 minutes.
User: I have placed the stuffed chicken inside the roasting pan and drizzled the olive oil and seasoned it with salt and pepper, what do I do next?
Robot: Now transfer the chicken to a serving platter and stir the lemon juice and 2 tbsp of parsley and the remaining 2 tbsp of butter inside the pan juice, scraping up any brown parts from the bottom.
User: I have moved the chicken to a serving platter and stirred the lemon juice and parsley, what do I do next?
Robot: Now, spoon the sauce over the chicken, serve and enjoy the meal.
