User: I would like to cook Lund’s Limpa Bread today, what are the ingredients required?
Robot: You will need the following ingredients, 2 pkgs of yeast, 1 and ½ cup of warm water, ¼ cup of dark molasses, ⅓ cup of granulated sugar, 1T of fine sea salt, 2T of lard, 1T of whole anise seeds, 1T of fresh grated orange zest, 2 and ½ cup of medium bohemian rye flour and 1 and ½ cup of white flour.
User: I have all of the required ingredients to make the bread, what is the first step?
Robot: Heat up a mixing bowl first of all, then add 1 and ½ cup of warm water and then dissolve the yeast with the molasses, ⅓ cup of granulated sugar, 1T of sea salt, 1T of fresh orange zest, whole anise seeds and the shortening mixed in together.
User: I have heated up the mixing bowl and added the required ingredients for shortening and mixed them, what do I do next?
Robot: Then, add 1 and ½ cups of rye flour and mix it until smooth.
User: I added the rye flour, what do I do next?
Robot: Then, add 2 and ½ cups of white flour a little at a time until the dough is kneaded well enough and a bit sticky to touch.
User: I added the white flour and it is now sticky to touch, what now?
Robot: Now, grease a large and warm bowl, then insert the dough into the bowl and turn the greased side facing upwards.
User:  I have greased the warm bowl and inserted the dough into the bowl, what do I do now?
Robot: Now cover it with a towel and then place the bowl in a warm place out of any draft.
User: I have covered it with a towel and placed it out of any draft, what is the next step?
Robot: Now you should let the covered dough rest and rise for approximately 60 minutes.
User:  I have let the dough rest and rise for 60 minutes, what is the next step?
Robot: When the dough has doubled in size, punch it down, round it and cover it and also let it rise for another 40 minutes more.
User:  I have punched the dough down, rounded it and covered it, what do I do next?
Robot: Now, punch the dough down again and divide the dough into 2 different pieces.
User:  I have divided the dough into 2 different pieces, what next?
Robot: Then, shape each piece into a round and slightly flat loaf.
User: I have shaped the piece into a round and flat loaf, what do I do next?
Robot: Now, place the two loaves on opposite ends of a greased baking sheet.
User: I have placed the two loaves on opposite ends of a baking sheet, what next?
Robot: Now cover the baking sheet with a towel and let them rise a third time, this time for 1 hour.
User: I have covered the baking sheet with a towel, what next?
Robot: Now, bake for 30-35 minutes at 375 degrees in a preheated oven.

Robot: Serve the meal and enjoy.
