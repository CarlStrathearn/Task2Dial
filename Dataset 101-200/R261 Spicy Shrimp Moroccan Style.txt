User: I would like to make Spicy Shrimp Moroccan Style, what are the ingredients required to make this meal?
Robot: For this meal you will need 2 pounds of uncooked shrimp, 4 peeled and crushed garlic cloves, ¼ cup of olive oil, 1 tsp of paprika, ½ tsp of cumin, ½ tsp of ground ginger, 1 pinch of cayenne pepper, sea salt, ¼ cup of Italian parsley, ¼ cup of chopped cilantro.
User: I have the ingredients ready, what is the first step that I need to take?
Robot: Good, fry the garlic in ¼ cup of olive oil over a medium heat until it turns light gold.
User: I have fried the garlic in olive oil over a medium heat, what do I do next?
Robot: Now, add the spices, salt and pepper and mix well with a spatula.
User: I have added the spices, salt and pepper and mixed with a spatula, what next?
Robot: Now, add 2 pounds of uncooked shrimp with the shells and tails removed and fry them over a medium for 8-10 minutes, stirring constantly, then add the chopped herbs and cook for another 2 minutes.
User: I have added the uncooked shrimp with the shells and tails, I also fried them for 10 minutes and stirred, what do I do now?
Robot: Now serve the meal on a hot bed of rice or with mixed green salad leaves, got that?
