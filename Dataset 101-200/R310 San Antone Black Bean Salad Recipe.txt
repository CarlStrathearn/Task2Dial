User: I would like to cook a San Antone Black Bean Salad, what ingredients do I need to make this Salad?
Robot: For this recipe you will need 2 lbs of black beans, 2 lbs of cooked sweet corn cut from the cob, 8 diced green onions, 2 cloves of garlic, 2-3 jalapeno peppers, 1 green bell pepper, 1 ripe avocado, 1 jar of pimentos, 3 seeded tomatoes, 1 cup of fresh cilantro, sea salt and cracked black pepper for the taste, those are the ingredients for the salad.
User: What about the ingredients for the dressing?
Robot: For the dressing you will need 3 tsp of fresh lime juice, 2 tsp of fresh orange juice, 2 and ½ tsp of lime zest, ½ tsp of ground cumin and black pepper to taste.
User: I have all of the ingredients ready to make the salad, what is the first step that I have to take?
Robot:Combine all of the salad ingredients in a large bowl, season with salt and pepper, then whisk the dressing vigorously to incorporate.
User: I have combined all of the salad ingredients into a bowl and seasoned them with salt and pepper, now what do I have to do?
Robot: Next, add the dressing to the salad and gently toss to combine everything together, then chill until ready to serve. You will also want to prepare the salad at least 4 hours prior to serving.
User: I have added the dressing to the salad and tossed everything together, what do I have to do now?
Robot: Next, sprinkle lime juice on top of the salad and continue to mix everything, then serve the salad and enjoy.
