R264 Easy Chicken Curry https://www.bbc.co.uk/food/recipes/simple_chicken_curry_95336

This recipe takes 10-30 minutes to cook, serves 4 and contains 11 ingredients,  [''spring onions'', ''garlic cloves'', '’vegetable oil'', '’chopped tomatoes'', ''curry powder'', ''ground ginger'’, ''chicken thighs'', ''yoghurt'’, ''salt'’, ''pepper'’, ''long grain rice'’]

User: Today I would like to make easy chicken curry, what are the ingredients required to make this meal?
Robot: You will need 6 spring onions, 3 garlic cloves, 2 tbsp of vegetable oil, half a tin of 400g chopped tomatoes, 2 tbsp of curry powder, 1 tsp of ground ginger, 400g of chicken thighs cut into 2.5cm pieces, 100ml of natural yoghurt, salt and pepper, also rice to serve.
User: I have all of the ingredients ready, what is the first step that I have to take?
Robot: Thinly slice 6 spring onions, reserving a handful of the sliced green parts for the garnish.
User: I have sliced the 6 onions, what do I do next?
Robot: Now, peel and chop 3 garlic cloves using a knife.
User: I have peeled and chopped 3 garlic cloves, what do I do now?
Robot: Heat 2 tbsp of vegetable oil in a large saucepan over a medium heat and cook the spring onions and the 3 garlic cloves for a few minutes.
User: I heated the vegetable oil in a large saucepan over medium heat and I cooked the onions and garlic, what do I do now?
Robot: Now, add 200g of chopped tomatoes, 2 tbsp of curry powder, 1 tsp of ground ginger and cook for 3-4 minutes, if the pan gets dry add some water and make sure that the spices don’t burn.
User: I have added 200g of tomatoes, curry powder and ginger, I also let it cook for 4 minutes, what do I do now?
Robot: Now, add 400g of chicken thighs and cook for 5 minutes and make sure that the chicken is coated and is beginning to brown on each side.
User: I have added 400g of chicken and I let it cook for 5 minutes, what do I do next?
Robot: Add 250ml of water and bring it to a boil, then reduce to a medium to low heat and then cook for 10-15 minutes more, or until the chicken is cooked through with no sign of pink juices.
User: I have added 250ml of water and brought it to a boil and reduced to a medium heat, what do I do next?
Robot: Now, while the chicken is still cooking, prepare 200g of long grain rice, then pour the rice into a saucepan and rinse it under the cold tap to take away any cloudy starch.
User: Thanks, I have prepared 200g of long grain rice and poured it in a saucepan, what next?
Robot: Now, add clean water for cooking the rice, then bring to the boil and reduce the heat to a lower simmer, got that?
User: I have added clean water for the rice and brought it to a boil and reduced it to a lower heat, what now?
Robot: Good, next cover with a lid and cook very gently for 10 minutes, then remove from the heat and leave it to stand with the lid on for 10 more minutes.
User: I covered it with a lid and cooked it for 10 minutes, then removed from the heat, what next?
Robot: Now, take the curry away from the heat, stir in 100ml of yoghurt then season with salt and pepper, then serve the curry with the rice and enjoy the meal.
