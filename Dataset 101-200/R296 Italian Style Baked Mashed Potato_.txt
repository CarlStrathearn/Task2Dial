R296 Italian Style Baked Mashed Potato https://www.whatscookinitalianstylecuisine.com/2010/03/italian-style-baked-mashed-potato.html

This recipe takes 30 minutes to cook, serves 3 and contains 15 ingredients,  [''mashed potatoes'', ''panko bread crumbs'', '’minced garlic'', '’garlic powder'', ''basil'', ''oregano'’, ''parsley'', ''salt'', ''black pepper'', ''cayenne pepper'', ''grating cheese'', ''olive oil'', ''aluminium foil'', ''wax paper'']

User: I would like to make an Italian Style Baked Mashed Potato, what is the first step that I need to take?
Robot: Take 1 cup of cold mashed potatoes and then add 1 clove of minced garlic and shape them into logs and place them into logs.
User: I have shaped the mashed potatoes and added the minced garlic, what next?
Robot: Place them on wax paper with a combined mixture of panko and Italian flavoured bread crumbs.
User: I have placed them on wax paper and combined with 1 package of panko bread crumbs, what next?
Robot: Then, add all of the spices and ¼ cup of grating cheese to the crumbs mix together and roll the logs into the crumbs to coat evenly, got that?
User: I have added the spices and the ¼ cup of grating cheese to the crumbs and rolled the logs into the crumbs, what is the next step that I have to take?
Robot: Now, on a cookie sheet lined with aluminium foil, add ¼ cup of olive oil and place the logs on the oil and bake the mashed potato logs in a hot preheated oven at 430 degrees until they go brown and crispy.
User: I have added the olive oil and placed the logs on the oil and mashed the logs in a preheated oven, what now?
Robot: Take them out of the oven, place the logs on a plate, serve hot and enjoy.
