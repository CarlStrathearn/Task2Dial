R254 Brussel Sprouts Sauteed in Garlic and Olive Oil https://cookeatshare.com/recipes/brussel-sprouts-sauteed-in-garlic-olive-oil-59

This recipe takes 10 minutes to cook, serves 6 and contains 5 ingredients,  [''brussel sprouts'', ''chopped garlic'', '’olive oil'', '’salt'', ''pepper'']

User: I would like to make Brussel Sprouts Sauteed in Garlic and Olive oil, what are the ingredients needed?
Robot: For this meal you will need 2 lbs of Brussel Sprouts, 1 tbsp of chopped garlic, ¼ cup of extra virgin oil, salt and pepper to taste.
User:   I have the ingredients ready, what is the first step I need to take?
Robot: Boil the Brussel Sprouts for 10-15 minutes depending on the desired crunchiness.
User: I have boiled the brussel sprouts, thank you, what do I do now?
Robot: Chop 1 tbsp of garlic while they are cooking, put ¼ cup of olive oil and the chopped garlic into a large saute pan.
User:   I have chopped the garlic and I put the olive oil into a large pan, what now?
Robot: Good, now remove the sprouts and put them into a large bowl, cover them with cool water.
User:  I have removed the sprouts and I put them in a large bowl, what now?
Robot: Good, now on a large cutting board, cut the sprouts in half.
User: I cut the sprouts in half, what now?
Robot: Saute the garlic over a medium heat until it is golden brown for approximately 1 minute.
User: Thank you very much, I saute the garlic over a medium heat for 1 minute, what do I do now?
Robot: Insert the sprouts in a pan and saute approximately 2 minutes, then turn the sides and saute for another 2 minutes.
User: I put the sprouts in a pan and saute for 2 minutes, what is the next step?
Robot: Add salt and pepper to taste, add some grated parmesan cheese if you wish, then serve and enjoy the meal.
Robot: Thank you for helping me cook Brussel Sprouts Sauteed in Garlic and Olive Oil.
