User: I would like to cook a chicken traybake with chorizo, tomato and red peppers. What is the first step that I need to take?
Robot: First preheat the oven to 200C at Gas 6.
User: I have preheated the oven, what next?
Robot: Now, put 2 quartered onions, 2 red peppers, 8 large tomatoes, 80g of chorizo, 400g of cannellini beans, 4 garlic cloves and 2 sprigs of rosemary in a large roasting tin.
User: I have put the onions, peppers, tomatoes, chorizo, beans, garlic and the rosemary in a large tin, what do I do next?
Robot: Now, top with the chicken and drizzle with 2 tbsp of olive oil.
User:  I have topped with the chicken and drizzled the oil, what next?
Robot: Scatter 1 tsp of sea salt flakes and a big pinch of pepper, then roast for 60 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the skin is golden brown.
User:  I have sprinkled the sea salt flakes and sprinkled pepper and also roasted for 60 minutes as required, what now?
Robot: Now, to check if the chicken is cooked, you must pierce the largest part of the chicken with a skewer and the juice should run clear.
User:  I have pierced the largest part with a skewer and the juice runs clear, what now?
Robot: Good, now leave the chicken to rest for 10 minutes, then serve the meal with vegetables and enjoy.
