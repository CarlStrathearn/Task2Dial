This recipe takes 20 minutes to cook, serves 4-6 and contains 15 ingredients,  [''pork tenderloin'', ''onion'', '’minced garlic cloves'', '’flat leaf parsley'', ''paprika'', ''hot red pepper flakes'’, ''ground cumin'', ''ground coriander'', ''dried oregano'', ''saffron threads'', ''olive oil'', ''wine vinegar'', ''dry white wine'', ''salt'', ''black pepper'']

User: I would like to cook BBQ Pork Tenderloin, what ingredients do I need to make this meal?
Robot: You will need 2 pounds of pork tenderloin, 1 medium onion finely diced, 3 cloves of minced garlic, 3 tbsp of minced flat-leaf parsley, 1 tbsp of paprika, ½ tsp of hot red pepper flakes, 1 tsp of ground cumin, ½ tsp of ground coriander, ½ tsp of dried oregano, ½ tsp of crumbled saffron threads, 3 tbsp of olive oil, 2 tbsp of red wine vinegar, 2 tbsp of dry white wine, 1 tsp of salt and ½ tsp of freshly ground black pepper.
User: I have the ingredients ready. What is the first step that I need to take?
Robot: Cut 2 pounds of tenderloin into 6 to 8 inch long pieces, make a few incisions in each side about ¼ inch deep and spread out about 1 inch apart for the marinade to penetrate.
User: I have cut the 2 pounds of tenderloin into the pieces and made incisions on every side, what do I have to do next?
Robot: Now, combine all of the other ingredients into a small bowl and stir, then put the tenderloin and marinade into a ziplock bag, squeeze out the air and zip.
User: I have combined all the other ingredients in a small brown and stirred and I also put the tenderloin and marinated in the ziplock bag, what do I do next?
Robot: Next, kneed with your hands to work the marinade into the pork, then refrigerate for approximately 6 hours or overnight.
User: I have marinated the pork and refrigerated for 6 hours as required, what next?
Robot: Now, preheat the grill to a medium high, then spray the grill or the tenderloin with a light spraying oil and put the tenderloin pieces on the grill and rotate every 3-4 minutes.
User: I have preheated the grill to a medium high and sprayed the grill with light spraying oil, what next?
Robot: Serve the meal on a plate and enjoy.
