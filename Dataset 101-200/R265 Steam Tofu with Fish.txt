User: I want to make Steam Tofu with Fish, what are the ingredients required to make this meal?
Robot: For this meal you will need 1 black soft tofu, a few slices of fish fillet, 1 tbsp of oil, 1 tsp of chopped garlic, 1 tsp of chopped ginger, 1 tbsp of oyster sauce, 1 tbsp of soy sauce, ½ tsp of sugar, 2 tbsp of water, a dash of pepper and spring onion for the garnishing.
User: I have all of the ingredients ready to make the meal, what is the first step?
Robot: Heat up 1 tbsp of oil and saute 1 tsp of ginger and 1 tsp of chopped garlic until fragrant.
User: Thanks, I have heated up the oil and sauteed the ginger, what do I have to do now?
Robot: Now, add the remaining ingredients and bring them to a boil.
User: I have added the remaining ingredients, what do I do now?
Robot: Arrange 1 block of tofu on a serving plate and then put the sliced fish on top.
User: I have put the tofu on a serving plate and put the sliced fish on top, what do I do now?
Robot: Good, now steam at a high heat for approximately 13 minutes.
User: I have steamed at a high heat for 13 minutes, what now?
Robot: Now, remove and pour away the excess water.
User: I have poured away the excess water, what next?
Robot: Pour over the sauce and garnish with the shredded spring onion on top and then serve the meal, enjoy.
