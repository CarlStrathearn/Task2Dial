User: I would like to cook a St Patrick’s Day Beef Stew with Guinness, what are the ingredients necessary to cook this meal?
Robot: For this meal you will need 3 cups of guinness, 1 tbsp of dijon mustard, a sprig of fresh rosemary, 2 bay leaves, 3 pounds of beef stew meat cut into cubes, 3 tbsp of canola oil, 4 tbsp of unsalted butter, 2 to 3 onions, 3 chopped stalks of celery, 3 peeled carrots, ½ pound of white mushrooms, 1 tbsp of all purpose flour, 1 minced garlic clove, salt and fresh ground pepper to taste and 2 tbsp of minced parsley.
User: I have all of the ingredients ready, what is the first step that I have to take?
Robot: In a large glass dish, combine 1 cup of Guinness, 1 tbsp of Dijon Mustard, a sprig of rosemary and 2 bay leaves.
User: I have combined the ingredients stated, what do I have to do now?
Robot: Now, add the beef cubes, stir to coat, cover and refrigerate them overnight.
User: I have added the beef cubes, stirred and covered them, I also left them to refrigerate overnight, what next?
Robot: Now, preheat the oven to 325 degrees fahrenheit, drain the meat and discard the marinade in a large skillet.
User: I have preheated the oven to 325 degrees, drained the meat and discarded the marinade in a large skillet, what do I have to do next?
Robot: Now, brown the meat in batches for approximately 3-5 minutes on each side, then transfer them to a large casserole dish.
User: I have browned the meat in batches for 5 minutes as necessary on each side and transferred them to a large dish, what do I have to do now?
Robot: Now, add 4 tbsp of unsalted butter to the skillet and when it starts to foam, add onions and cook for 5 minutes or until they go all brown.
User: I have added the butter to the skillet and I added the onions and let them cook for 5 minutes.
Robot: Add 3 stalks of celery and 3 carrots and let them cook, make sure you stir constantly for 5 minutes, or until they go all tender, then reduce the heat to simmer.
User: I have added the celery and carrots, what do I have to do next?
Robot: Next, add the remaining 2 cups of Guinness, garlic, salt and pepper and occasionally add a little bit more Guinness if the stew becomes dry.
User: I have added the remaining 2 cups of Guinness, garlic, salt and pepper, what do I do next?
Robot: Adjust the seasonings and sprinkle with the chopped parsley, serve and enjoy the meal.
