User: I want to make Chicken Cordon Bleu, what ingredients do I need for this recipe?
Robot: In order to make Chicken Cordon Bleu you will need ½ cup of olive oil, 1 and ¼ pounds of thin sliced boneless chicken breast, 5 ounces of sliced ham, 4 ounces of sliced swiss cheese, 2 large beaten eggs, 1 to 2 cups of Japanese style bread crumbs and lemon wedges, got that?
User: I have all of the ingredients needed to make Chicken Cordon Bleu, where do I begin?
Robot: Firstly, heat ¼ cup of olive oil in a large flat skillet over a medium heat, then place 2 large beaten eggs and the bread crumbs in a separate wide flat bowl, then set aside.
User:  I have heated the olive oil in a large flat skillet over medium heat and I placed the eggs in a bowl and set them all aside. What do I have to do now?
Robot: Good, now select and pair up the chicken breast cutlets into equal size, then place between a wax paper.
User: I have paired up the chicken breasts into equal sizes and placed them between a wax paper, what do I do now?
Robot: Good, now put a slice of ham on one of the thinned chicken cutlets and on top of that a slice of swiss cheese, then trim the ham and cheese to fit the cutlet shape.
User: I added the ham onto the thinned chicken cutlets and I then trimmed the ham and cheese, what now?
Robot:Now dip them into the beaten eggs in order to coat one side completely and then carefully turn it over and dip the second side in eggs.
User: I dipped them into the beaten eggs and they are coated perfectly, what do I have to do now?
Robot: Now, coat both of the sides with bread crumbs.
User: I have coated both of the sides with the bread crumbs, what next?
Robot: Then place them into heated oil in a pan and then fry them until they go golden brown on both of the sides and then add an additional ¼ cup of oil.
User: I placed them into heated oil and they are now golden brown, what do I do next?
Robot: Now, simply serve with lemon wedges and enjoy the meal.
