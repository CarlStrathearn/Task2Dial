User: I want to make Caribbean Turkey Wings, what ingredients do I need?
Robot:  to make Caribbean Turkey Wings you will need ¼ cup of pineapple juice, ¼ cup of brown sugar that is firmly packed, 4 chopped scallions, 4 chopped garlic cloves, 1 tbsp of vegetable oil, 1 tbsp of distilled white vinegar, 1 tsp of ground allspice, ½ tsp of cayenne pepper, ¼ tsp of salt, ¼ tsp of freshly ground black pepper, 4 and ½ pounds of turkey wings, got it?
User: I have the ingredients ready to make the turkey wings, what is the first step?
Robot: Place all of the ingredients except the turkey wings in a food processor or a blender.
User:  I have placed the ingredients except the turkey wings in a food processor, what next?
Robot: Process them until they are all thoroughly mixed, then cut off the wing tips and reserve for another use.
User: They have been processed and have been thoroughly mixed, I also cut off the wing tips, what is the next step?
Robot: Now, cut the remaining wings into 2 pieces at a joint using a knife and place them in a container. Then, pour the mixture over the wings, cover and marinate for approximately 60 minutes in a refrigerator.
User:  I have cut the remaining wings and poured the mixture over the wings, covered and marinated for 60 minutes as required, what do I have to do now?
Robot: Good, now transfer onto an oven tray, cover it with foil and bake in a preheated 375 degree fahrenheit oven for about 1 hour, basting occasionally.
User: I covered it with foil and baked it in a 375 degree oven for 1 hour. What comes next?
Robot: Now remove the foil and bake uncovered for an additional 15 minutes.
User:  I have removed the foil and baked uncovered for 15 minutes, now what?
Robot: Put the meal on a plate, serve and enjoy.
