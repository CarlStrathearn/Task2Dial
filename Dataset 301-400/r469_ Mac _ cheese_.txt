This recipe takes 30 minutes to prepare, 30-60 minutes to cook, serves 6-8 and contains 14 ingredients,  ['butter', 'plain flour', 'dijon mustard', 'full fat milk', 'double cream’, 'bay leaf', 'mature cheddar’,'grated nutmeg', 'dried macaroni', 'smoked bacon lardons', 'parmesan', 'breadcrumbs’, 'salt', 'pepper']

User: I would like to make Mac & Cheese
Robot: First preheat the oven to 200ºC and grease a 35x20cm ovenproof dish.
User: The oven has heated up and I have greased the dish, what do I do now?
Robot: Now melt 100g of butter in a large saucepan over a low heat. Stir in 100g of plain flour and cook for 1 minute. Add in 1tsp Dijon mustard and take the pan off the heat, gradually whisking in 1.2L of milk and 75ml of double cream. Add the bay leaf to this. 
User: I have stirred in those ingredients and added a bay leaf, what now?
Robot: Now, put the pan back on the heat and stir constantly until it boils and thickens. Take off the heat again and remove the bay leaf. Now using a grater, grate 400g of mature cheddar and stir into the pan until melted. Season with black pepper and a pinch of nutmeg. 
User: I have stirred those together into a thick sauce. What comes next?
Robot: Next, bring a large pan of salted water to the boil and stir in 500g of macaroni. Cook for 10 minutes and drain well, adding it to the sauce. 
User: I have cooked the pasta, what next?
Robot: Next, fry 100g of smoked bacon lardons in a frying pan until crisp. Stir this into the macaroni cheese and pour the mixture into the ovenproof dish. Next mix 60g of breadcrumbs with 50g of parmesan and scatter this over the top. 
User: The lardons are finished and in the pan with the rest of it. What now?
Robot: Now bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown and bubbling. Once complete, serve and enjoy
