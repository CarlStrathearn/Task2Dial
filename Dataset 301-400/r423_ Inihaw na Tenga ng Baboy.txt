This recipe takes 20 minutes to prepare, 90 minutes to cook, serves 1 and contains 10 ingredients,  ['pork ears', 'vinegar', 'soy sauce', 'garlic', 'bay leaves’, 'peppercorns', 'salt’,'bamboo skewers', 'bottled vinaigrette', 'dark brown sugar']

User: I would like to make Inihaw na Tenga ng Baboy
Robot: First clean and trim 2 pork ears before cutting them into small pieces using a knife. Place the cut pork ears in a pan and pour in 180ml of vinegar over them. Add 1 whole garlic, peeled and crushed with a garlic press; 2 bay leaves, and 15g of peppercorns. Bring this to a boil without stirring, leave it for a few minutes boiling before stirring. 
User: I have cut up the pork ears and added it to the pan with the other ingredients. What now?
Robot: Now season the pan with some salt and add 120ml of soy sauce before pouring in 240ml of water and simmer for 1-1.5 hours or until the pork ears are tender. 
User: The pork ears are tender, what comes next?
Robot: Drain the pork ears and allow them to cool. After cooling, thread them into bamboo skewers. 
User: I have threaded the pork ears onto the skewers after cooling. What is the next step?
Robot: The next step is to get a small bowl and use a spoon to mix 160 ml of vinaigrette and 160g of firmly packed dark brown sugar together to make the basting sauce. 
User: I have made the basting sauce, what next?
Robot: Next apply the basting sauce to the pork ears by using a cooking brush, or rolling the skewers in the sauce. Then, grill the pork on the skewers making sure it is at least 20cm away from the heat. Grill until cooked and serve with vinegar as the dipping sauce.
