User: I would like to make Low-Fat Spice Rubbed Roast Turkey.
Robot: First preheat the oven to 220ºC. In a small bowl add 60g of brown sugar, 30g of smoked paprika, 30g of ground cinnamon, 15g of kosher salt, 15g of garlic powder, 15g of onion powder, and 15 g of dried oregano. Combine these together and rub them all over a 5.5kg turkey. 
User: I have seasoned the turkey, what next?
Robot: Next tie the turkey legs together with a string and place them on the rack of a roasting pan. Roast the turkey for 40 minutes and reduce the oven to 190ºC, loosely tenting the top of the turkey with foil. Continue roasting until a meat thermometer reads 70ºC when probed, another 1-1.5 hours. 
User: I have roasted the seasoned turkey in the oven and it is now cooked. What now?
Robot: Now let the turkey rest for 30 minutes, carve and enjoy.
