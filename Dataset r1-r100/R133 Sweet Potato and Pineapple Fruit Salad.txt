User: Today I want to make a Sweet Potato and Pineapple Fruit Salad, what ingredients do I need?
Robot: Ok, you will need 1 large sweet potato, 5-6 slices of kiwi, 1 fresh diced pineapple, 2 diced peaches, ¼ cup of shredded coconut and ¼ cup of coconut milk.
User: I have the ingredients ready, am I ready to begin or do I need any other ingredients?
Robot: You are ready to start. Wash the sweet potato and poke it all over with a fork, then place it on a medium plate and microwave it until it becomes soft.
User: I have washed the sweet potatoes and poked it with a fork, what now?
Robot: Now, press with your finger for approximately 10 minutes, then allow them to cool down and slice them off with a sharp knife.

Robot: Now, dice the potatoes into smaller bite-sized pieces and place them in a medium bowl, did you get that?
User:  I have diced the potatoes into smaller pieces and put them in a medium bowl, what do I do now?
Robot: Now lightly toast the shredded coconut in a very small skillet over a medium heat, make sure to stir frequently for about 5 minutes.

Robot: Next, add the toasted coconut and the coconut milk to a bowl and toss it to combine, then serve immediately or chill and serve.
