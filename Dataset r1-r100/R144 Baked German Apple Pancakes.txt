User: I have the ingredients ready, am I ready to start or do I need any other ingredients?
Robot: You do not need any other ingredients, you are ready to begin. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees fahrenheit, then beat 4 eggs with an electric hand mixer on a medium speed until it becomes foamy for about 2 minutes.

Robot: Now add ½ cup of milk and 2 tbsp of whipping cream, beat until well blended for about 1 minute, then set aside.

Robot: Now sift ¾ cups of bread flour, 1 tbsp of confectioners’ sugar, 1 and ½ tsp of wheat gluten, ¼ tsp of salt and ⅛ tsp of nutmeg together in a medium bowl.
User: I have sifted the ingredients together in a medium bowl, what is the next step that I have to take?
Robot: Now, add one third of the flour mixture to the reserved egg mixture and beat on a low speed to combine, for approximately 2 minutes.
User: I added ⅓ of the flour mixture to the reserved egg mixture and beat it on a low speed to combine. What is the next step that I have to take?
Robot:Next, let the batter rest for about 15 minutes, then continue with preparation for the apples and the cinnamon topping.

Robot: In order to make the apples, melt the butter in a 9-inch non-stick-oven-safe skillet over a medium heat, then cook the apple slices in a skillet, turning occasionally until the apples begin to soften and sweat.

Robot:To make the cinnamon topping, mix 4 tbsp of dark brown sugar, 3 tbsp of superfine sugar and ½ tsp of cornstarch in a small bowl with a fork.
User: I mixed the ingredients in a small bowl with a fork, what is the next step that I have to take?
Robot: Slowly pour 3 tbsp of butter into the cinnamon-sugar mixture, stirring with a fork until it has completely mixed.

Robot: Spread the cinnamon sugar mixture over the bottom of the now-empty skillet with a spatula.
