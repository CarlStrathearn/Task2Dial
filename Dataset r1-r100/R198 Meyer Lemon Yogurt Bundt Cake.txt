User: Today I feel like baking a Meyer Lemon Yogurt Bundt Cake, what are the required ingredients to make this cake?
Robot: For this recipe you will need the following ingredients, 1 and a ½ cups of all-purpose flour, 2 teaspoons of baking powder, ½ teaspoon of kosher salt, 1 cup of plain whole-milk yogurt, 1 and ⅓ cups of divided sugar, 3 extra-large eggs, 2 teaspoons of grated lemon-zest, ½ teaspoon of pure vanilla extract, ½ cup of vegetable oil, ⅓ cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice.
User: I have the ingredients ready for the cake, what about the glazing?
Robot: For the glazing you will need the following ingredients, 1 cup of confectioners’ sugar, 2 tablespoons of freshly squeezed lemon juice and low-fat greek yogurt.

Robot: Firstly, you have to preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit, then grease an 8 and ½ by 4 and ¼ by 2 and 1/2 -inch loaf pan.

Robot: Then, line the bottom with parchment paper, grease and flour the pan.

Robot: Sift 1 and ½ cups of all-purpose flour, 2 teaspoons of baking powder and ½ teaspoon of kosher salt all into 1 bowl.

Robot: Then, in another bowl, whisk together 1 cup of whole-milk yogurt, 1 cup of sugar, the 3 extra-large eggs, 2 teaspoons of lemon zest and ½ teaspoon of vanilla extract.

Robot: After that, slowly whisk the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients.
