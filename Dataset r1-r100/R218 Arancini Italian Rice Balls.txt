User: I would like to make Arancini Italian Rice balls today, what ingredients do I need to make them?
Robot:  In order to make Arancini Italian Rice balls, you will need the following ingredients, 1 finely minced onion, 4 tbsp of butter or margarine, 2 cups of arborio rice, 4 cups of chicken broth, ½ cup of grated pecorino romano or parmesan cheese and these ingredients are for the risotto.
User:  I have the ingredients for the risotto, what about the filling?
Robot:  You will need the following ingredients for the filling, 1 small finely diced onion, 1 small grated carrot, 1 small piece of finely diced celery, 1 garlic clove that is smashed to a pulp, 1 pound of ground beef, 1 cup of marinara sauce, 1 tsp of finely minced basil, 1 tsp of minced parsley, salt and pepper to taste, 1 cup of thawed frozen baby green peas and 1 tbsp of olive oil.
User: I have the ingredients ready for the filling, what about the ingredients for the coating?
Robot: For the coating, you will need 1 cup of all purpose flour, 1 cup of plain bread crumbs, ½ cup of grated Pecorino Romano or Parmesan Cheese and 3 eggs.

Robot: Then, once the rice has cooled add the grated cheese.

Robot: Then, cover set in the fridge until it’s cold, the rice can be done the day before.
