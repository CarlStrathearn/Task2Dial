Robot: In a large bowl with an electric mixer at hand, beat 2 sticks of butter, 1 and ½ cups of light brown sugar and 2 tsp of vanilla until they become light and fluffy, do this for approximately 1 minute.

Robot: Next, add 2 large eggs and mix them until they are thoroughly incorporated.

Robot: Now, add 2 cups of quick oats, 1 and ½ cups of flour, 1 cup of shredded coconut, 1 tsp of baking powder, 1 tsp of ground cinnamon, ½ tsp of nutmeg and ½ tsp of salt, then mix them all on a low speed until they blend well.
User: I have added the ingredients and mixed them well, they are now blended well, can you please tell me what I have to do next?
Robot: Next, add 1 cup of blueberries and fold them in gently by hand and drop the batter by heaping tablespoons 1 and ½ inch apart onto greased baking sheets.
User: I added the blueberries and folded them in by hand as required, what is the next step?
Robot: Now bake them until they turn golden brown for about 15-18 minutes, the cookies will start to feel soft but they will start to become firm as they cool down.

Robot: Now, transfer the cookies to the rack to cool down, then serve them on a plate and enjoy.
