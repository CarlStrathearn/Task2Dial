Robot: Now, cook the ingredients over a medium heat until the butter is melted, do this for approximately 7 minutes, then pour the milk mixture over the bread and let it stand for 10 minutes.

Robot: Now stir in all of the remaining ingredients and pour them into a greased 1 and ½ quarter casserole, then you will need to bake them for 50 minutes or until they set in the center.
