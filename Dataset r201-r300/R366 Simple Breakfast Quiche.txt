R366 Simple Breakfast Quiche https://cookeatshare.com/recipes/simple-breakfast-quiche-642

This recipe takes 35 minutes to cook, serves 8 and contains 8 ingredients,  [''bacon'', ''Swiss cheese'', '’butter'', '’eggs'', ''chopped onions'', ''salt'’, ''all-purpose flour'', ''milk'']

User: I want to cook a Simple Breakfast Quiche, what ingredients do I need to get started?
Robot: For this meal you will need the following ingredients, 8 slices of bacon, 4 ounces of shredded Swiss cheese, 2 tbsp of melted butter, 4 beaten eggs, ¼ cup of finely chopped onions, 1 tsp of salt, ¼ cup of all-purpose flour and 1 and ½ cups of milk.
User: I have the ingredients ready, am I ready to start or do I need any other ingredients?
Robot: No, you do not need anymore ingredients, you are ready to begin. Firstly, place 8 slices of bacon in a large and deep skillet and cook them over a medium high heat until they turn brown.
User: I placed the bacon slices in a skillet and they are now brown and crispy. What do I need to do next?
Robot: Now drain, crumble and set them aside, after that preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit and lightly grease a 9 inch pie pan.
User: I put the bacon slices aside and preheated the oven to 350 degrees. What is the next step that I need to take?
Robot:Line the bottom of the pie plate with 4 ounces of shredded Swiss cheese and crumbled bacon.
User: I lined the bottom of the pie plate with the cheese and crumbled bacon, what do I need to do next?
Robot:Combine 4 beaten eggs, 2 tbsp of butter, ¼ cup of finely chopped onions, 1 tsp of salt and ½ cup of milk, then whisk them together until smooth.
User: I combined the eggs with the rest of the ingredients and whisked them together, now the mixture is all smooth, what next?
Robot: Now, pour the mixture into the pie pan and brush the egg white on the trim of the crust, then bake in the preheated oven for 35 minutes until it sets.
User: I poured the mixture into the pie pan and brushed the egg whites on the trims of the crust. What do I need to do next after I let it cook for 35 minutes?
Robot: Serve the quiche hot or cold on a plate and enjoy.
