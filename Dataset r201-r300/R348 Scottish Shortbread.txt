User: I have the ingredients ready. Am I ready to start?
Robot: You can start. Sift or whisk the dry ingredients into a bowl, then cut ¼ sticks of butter into pieces and then add it to the bowl.

Robot: Next with your hands knead into a firm dough, then place the dough on a floured board and roll it into ⅓ inch thickness.
User: I have placed the dough on a board and rolled it into the required dimension, what is the next step?
Robot: Cut the dough with a 2-inch round cookie cutter or with a sharp knife into small triangles or finger shapes and after that place it on an ungreased cookie sheet.
User: I have cut the dough with a cookie cutter into small triangles and placed it on an ungreased cookie sheet. What is the next step that I have to take?
Robot: Prick each cookie three times with a toothpick or with a fork and sprinkle ¼ cup of granulated sugar over the top and bake at 375 degrees fahrenheit.
User: I have pricked the cookies with a fork and sprinkled over the sugar on top, I also let it cook for 375 degrees as required, could you please tell me what I have to do next?
Robot: Now after the cookies have cooked, remove them from the oven and while they are still hot sprinkle the tops with some more granulated sugar, then let it cool down in an airtight container at room temperature.
