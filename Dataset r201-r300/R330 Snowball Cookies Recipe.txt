User: I would like to make Snowball Cookies, what ingredients do I need to make the cookies?
Robot: You will need 2 cups of all-purpose flour, 2 cups of finely chopped pecans, ¼ cup of granulated sugar, 1 cup of softened butter, 1 tsp of vanilla and powdered sugar for the rolling.
User: Are there any other ingredients that I need or am I ready to start?
Robot: You are ready to start. First, preheat the oven to 325 degrees and in a large mixing bowl combine 2 cups of flour, 2 cups of pecans, ¼ cup of granulated sugar, ¼ cup of softened butter and 1 tsp of vanilla.
User: I preheated the oven to 325 degrees in a large mixing bowl and combined the flour, 2 cups of pecans, granulated sugar, 1 cup of butter and the 1 tsp of vanilla, what next?
Robot: Next, mix at a very low speed, scraping the bowl often until they are well mixed for about 5 minutes, then shape the rounded teaspoons of dough into 1 inch balls.
User: I mixed at a very low speed and scraped the bowl for 5 minutes as required. What next?
Robot: Now, place them on ungreased cookie sheets and bake them for 25 minutes or until they go light brown.
User: I have placed them on cookie sheets and baked them for 25 minutes, what do I do next?
Robot: Remove them immediately and roll them in powdered sugar while the cookies are still hot, then place them on cooling racks.
User: I have removed them and rolled them in powdered sugar, what do I have to do next?
Robot: Roll them in powdered sugar once again and make about 3 dozen of them, put them on a plate and serve them, enjoy.
