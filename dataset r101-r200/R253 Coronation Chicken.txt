User: I want to cook Coronation Chicken, what ingredients do I need?
Robot: You will need 1.5kg of free-range chicken, 4 sliced spring onions, 6 garlic cloves, 1 tbsp of sea salt, 10 whole black peppercorns, for the sauce you will need 1 tbsp of vegetable oil, 1 chopped onion, 1 tbsp of curry powder, 1 tbsp of tomato puree, 85ml of red wine, 150ml of water, 1 bay leaf, salt and freshly ground black pepper, caster sugar, 2 slices of lemon, lemon juice, 425ml of mayonnaise, 2 tbsp of apricot puree, 3 tbsp of whipped cream.
User: I have the ingredients ready, where do I begin?
Robot: Place 1.5kg of chicken into a saucepan with a tight-fitting lid on it, cover it with water and add 4 spring onions, 6 peeled garlic cloves, salt and 10 whole black peppercorns.
User:  I have added the chicken, onions, garlic and salt, what do I have to do next?
Robot: Now, bring the ingredients to a boil and simmer for approximately 30 minutes, turning the chicken once during the cooking process.
User:  I have brought the ingredients to a boil and simmered for 30 minutes, what do I do now?
Robot: Now, cover with a lid and switch off the heat, then leave for 1 hour, remove the chicken and allow it to cool completely.
User:  I have covered the lid and switched off the heat, left it for 1 hour and removed the chicken, what next?
Robot: For the sauce, heat 1 tbsp of vegetable oil in a pan over a medium heat and then add 1 chopped onion and gently cook for 3-4 minutes.
User:   I have heated 1 tbsp of vegetable oil and added 1 chopped onion, what now?
Robot: Add 1 tbsp of tomato puree, 85ml of red wine, 150ml of water and 1 bay leaf, then bring the mixture to a boil.
User:   I have added the tomato puree, red wine, water and the bay leaf, what do I do next?
Robot: Season with salt and ground black pepper, then add caster sugar, 2 slices of lemon and a squeeze of lemon juice.
User:   I have seasoned it with salt and black pepper and added the sugar, what next?
Robot: Reduce the heat until the mixture is simmering and let it simmer uncovered for 5-10 minutes.
User:  I have reduced the heat and the mixture has simmered for 10 minutes, what next?
Robot: Good, now strain the sauce through a fine meshed sieve and set it aside to cool down.
User:  I have strained the sauce through a sieve and set it aside to cool down, what now?
Robot: Now, gradually fold in the mayonnaise and 2 tbsp of apricot puree, add some more lemon juice if necessary.
User:  I have folded the mayonnaise and the puree, what next?
Robot: Now, fold in the whipped cream and to serve, arrange the portioned chicken on a large platter, pour the sauce over the top, sprinkle with 1 tbsp of coriander and serve immediately with rice.
