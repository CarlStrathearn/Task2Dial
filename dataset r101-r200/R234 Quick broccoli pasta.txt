This recipe takes 10-30 minutes to cook, serves 2 and contains 10 ingredients,  [''rigatoni'', ''spring onions'', '’garlic clove'', '’cherry tomatoes'', ''broccoli'', ''vegetable oil'’, ''chili flakes'', ''cream cheese'', ''salt'', ''pepper'']

User: I would like to cook a quick broccoli pasta, what are the required ingredients for this meal?
Robot: In order to make quick broccoli pasta, you will need 150g of rigatoni or penne pasta, 3 spring onions, 1 garlic clove, 125g of cherry tomatoes, 150g of broccoli, 1 tbsp of vegetable oil, a pinch of chili flakes, 75g of cream cheese, salt and pepper.
User: I have all of the ingredients ready to make the pasta, what is the first step that I need to take?
Robot: Have a large pan ready, add salted water in it to boil, then add 150g of pasta and let it cook for approximately 12 minutes.
User: I have added the salted water in the pan to boil, I also added 150g of pasta and let it cook for 12 minutes, what do I do next?
Robot: Next, you should slice 3 spring onions, peel them and finely grate 1 garlic clove at the same time, then halve 125g of cherry tomatoes and thinly slice the broccoli florets and stalks.
User: I have sliced the 3 spring onions, I peeled them and grated the 1 garlic clove and halved the tomatoes, what is the next step that I need to take?
Robot: Now, heat 1 tbsp of vegetable oil in a large, deep frying pan and add the spring onions, then let them fry for 1 minute until they are completely soft.
User: I have added 1 tbsp of vegetable oil in a deep frying pan, I also added the spring onions and fried them for 1 minute, what now?
Robot: Add the 1 garlic clove, a pinch of chilli flakes and fry for 1 minute.
User: I have added the garlic clove and a pinch of chili flakes and fried for 1 minute, what is the next step?
Robot: Add the tomatoes, fry them for a couple of minutes and when they are broken down then add the broccoli, then fry for 2 minutes and add a ladle or a small mug of the pasta water, give everything a stir and cover with a lid.
User: I have added the tomatoes, fried them for a couple of minutes and then I added the broccoli, further fried them for 2 minutes and gave everything a stir and covered with a lid, what is the next step?
Robot: Then cook for 3-4 minutes until the broccoli has finished cooking, then remove the lid.
User: I have cooked for another 3-4 minutes and the broccoli has finished cooking, I also removed the lid, what do I do now?
Robot: You then have to drain the pasta, add to the pan with the broccoli and stir to combine.
User: I have drained the pasta and added it to the pan with the broccoli, what now?
Robot: Season with salt and pepper, then stir through the cream cheese and add a bit more black pepper if you wish to do so, serve the meal and enjoy.
