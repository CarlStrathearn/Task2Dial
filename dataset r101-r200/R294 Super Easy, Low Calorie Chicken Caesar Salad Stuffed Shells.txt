R294 Super Easy, Low Calorie Chicken Caesar Salad Stuffed Shells https://cookeatshare.com/recipes/super-easy-low-calorie-chicken-caesar-salad-stuffed-shells-680245

This recipe takes 10 minutes to cook, serves 12 and contains 6 ingredients,  [''pasta shells'', ''romaine lettuce'', '’tomatoes'', '’chicken'', ''parmesan cheese'', ''caesar dressing'’]

User: I want to cook a Low Calorie Chicken Caesar Salad Stuffed Shells today, what are the ingredients required to make this meal?
Robot: You will need 12 jumbo pasta shells, 2 cups of romaine lettuce, ½ cup of diced tomatoes, ½ cup of chicken, 2 tbsp of parmesan cheese and 2 tbsp of light caesar dressing.
User: I have the ingredients ready, what is the first step that I have to take?
Robot: To cook the shells, drain and rinse in cold water and drain again, then blot them dry with paper towels and set the shells aside on a plate.
User: I have drained and rinsed the shells and I also put them aside, what is the next step?
Robot: Now, take a medium bowl and put all of the ingredients apart from the cooked pasta shells and mix them well.
User: I have put all of the ingredients in the bowl and mixed them well enough, what next?
Robot: Next, stuff each of the shells with 1 overflowing tablespoon, serve the shells on a plate and enjoy.
