User: I have the ingredients ready, where do I begin?
Robot: Clean 3 lbs of fresh spinach and transfer it into a pot with about 3-4 cups of water and 1 tsp of salt added.

Robot: Add 3 tbsp of butter and ½ chopped medium onion in a large soup pot and turn it to a medium heat.

Robot: Now add the broth, 1 cup of low fat milk, ⅛ tsp of nutmeg and bring to a simmer by stirring occasionally, then add 5 tbsp of grated parmesan cheese, taste and correct for salt.
