This recipe takes 120 minutes to cook, serves 6 and contains 2 ingredients,  [''sirloin tip roast'', ''red wine'']

User: I would like to make a Smoked Sirloin Tip Roast today, what ingredients do I need?
Robot: For this meal you will need 5 and ½ pounds of sirloin tip roast and 1 and ½ cups of red wine.
User: I have the ingredients ready, what is the first step that I have to take?
Robot: Firstly, season the steak with your favourite seasoning, then put it in an oven safe dish, pour in 1 and ½ cups of red wine and smoke it for about an hour at approximately 150 degrees.
User: I seasoned the steak and put it in an oven safe dish, I also smoked it for 150 degrees. What do I have to do next?
Robot: Now, baste the steak every now and then and then preheat the grill grates at around 205 degrees celsius for cleaning the burn.
User: I have basted the steak and preheated the grill grates at 205 degrees, what next?
Robot: After cleaning the grates, turn it back down to 150 degrees and place everything directly on the grill and just let it hang out in the smoke.
User: I have turned it back down to 150 degrees and placed everything on the grill, what next?
Robot: Next, after the steak has finished cooking, put it on a warm plate, serve with wine and enjoy the meal.
