R259 Taro Root https://cookeatshare.com/recipes/taro-root-881590

This recipe takes 30 minutes to cook, serves 2 and contains 4 ingredients,  [''taro roots'', ''water'', '’salt'', '’yogurt'']

User: I would like to make Taro Root, what ingredients do I need?
Robot: You will need 8 small taro roots, water, salt and 1 cup of yogurt mixed with enough water to submerge the roots.
User: I have all of the ingredients ready to start, what is the first step?
Robot: Wash the 8 taro roots very well, then slightly cut a slit into each root using a knife, but not big enough to split the root into two pieces.
User: I have washed the roots and cut them a slit into each root, what now?
Robot: Place the taro roots in an Instant Pot and submerge them with heavy salted water, then close the lid and set the vent to sealing.
User: I have placed the roots in an instant pot and submerged them with water, what do I have to do next?
Robot: Now set the pot to cook for 4 minutes at a high pressure, serve and enjoy the meal.
