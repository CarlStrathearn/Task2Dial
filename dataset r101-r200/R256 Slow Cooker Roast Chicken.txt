User: I would like to cook Slow Cooker Roast Chicken today, what ingredients do I need?
Robot: You will need 1 sliced onion, 1.6kg of a whole chicken, 1 tbsp of sunflower oil, 1 tsp of paprika, 1 tsp of flaked sea salt, ½ tsp of ground black pepper.
User: I have all of the ingredients ready to make the Chicken, where do I begin?
Robot: Scatter 1 thick onion over the base of a slow cooker, remove any trussing string from the chicken and place breast-side up on the top.

Robot: Pour the oil into a smaller bowl, stir 1 tsp of paprika, salt and pepper, then brush the oil over the chicken.
User: I poured the oil in a smaller bowl and stirred the paprika, what do I do next?
Robot: Cover the slow cooker with the lit and cook on a high for 4 and ½ hours or until the chicken is tender and thoroughly cooked.
User: I have covered the slow cooker and let it cook for 4 and ½ hours, what do I do now?
Robot: Good, now remove the lid and pierce the thickest part of the bird with a skewer and make sure the juices run clear.
User: I have removed the lid and pierced the thickest part, what now?
Robot: Transfer the chicken to a board and let it rest.
