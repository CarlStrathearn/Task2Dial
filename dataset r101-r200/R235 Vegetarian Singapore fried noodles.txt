This recipe takes less than 10 minutes to cook, serves 2 and contains 10 ingredients,  [''vermicelli noodles'', ''red onion'', '’carrot'', '’spring onions'', ''green cabbage'', ''vegetable oil'’, ''curry powder'', ''chili powder'', ''soy sauce'', ''sesame oil'']

User: I would like to make Vegetarian Singapore fried noodles, what are the ingredients required to make this meal?
Robot: To make vegetarian singapore fried noodles you will need the following ingredients ready, 100g of dried rice vermicelli noodles, ½ medium red onion that is finely sliced, 1 large carrot, peeled and cut into thin matchsticks, 1 small leek, or 3 spring onions, trimmed and cut, 2-3 leaves of green cabbage, or spring greens, 1 and ½ tbsp of vegetable oil, fresh chives or sliced spring onions to serve.
User: I have the ingredients ready, what about the ingredients for the curry paste?
Robot: For the curry paste you will need the following ingredients: 2 tsp of medium curry powder, ¼ - ½ tsp of hot chili powder for taste, 1 tbsp of light soy sauce and ½ tsp of sesame oil.
User: I have these ingredients ready, now what is the first step that I need to take?
Robot: Place 100g of vermicelli noodles in a large bowl and then cover with just boiled water.
User:  I have placed 100g of noodles in a large bowl and covered it with boiling water. What now?
Robot: After that, leave them to stand for 2 minutes, then drain them in a sieve and spread them out onto a clean tea towel.
User:  I have left them to stand for 2 minutes and drained them on a sieve, what do I do next?
Robot: Now, pat and press until they are dry as much as possible, then transfer them over to a bowl.
User: I have pressed them down and they are now dry and also moved them to a bowl, what is the next step?
Robot: Leave the noodles to dry down on a second clean towel for approximately 10-15 minutes before putting them into the bowl, this way it will help them stay al dente and separate as they cook.
User: I have left the noodles to dry on a clean towel for 10 minutes, what next?
Robot: You should then mix together all of the ingredients for the curry paste in a smaller bowl until thoroughly combined.
User: I have mixed the ingredients for the curry paste in a smaller bowl, what next?
Robot: Then, place the prepared vegetables in a semi-circle on a board or plate, starting with the onion and ending with the shredded leaves.
User: I have placed the vegetables on a plate with the onion and ending with the leaves. What is the next step that I have to take?
Robot: Now arrange the bowl of noodles and the paste below to give a wheel of ingredients.
User: I have arranged the bowl of noodles and the paste to give a wheel of ingredients, what next?
Robot: Now, heat ½ tbsp of the oil in a wok over a very high heat.
User: I have heated ½ tbsp of oil in a wok over a high heat, what do I do now?
Robot: Now add ½ medium red onion and then stir-fry for approximately 10-15 seconds, then add the carrot and stir-fry for another 10 seconds more.
User: I have added the red onion and the carrot and I stir fried for the amount of time necessary, what next?
Robot: Now, add the leek and stir-fry for another 10 seconds more before adding a bit more oil and the leaves.
User: I have stir fried for another 10 seconds and added the oil and leaves, what next?
Robot: Then, stir-fry for another 10-15 seconds more, move all of the vegetables to the side of the wok, leaving the centre clear.
User: I have moved all the vegetables to the side of the wok and the centre is now clear, what next?
Robot: Add another teaspoon of oil, wait for the wok to be smoking hot, then add the noodles directly in the middle of the wok.
User:  I have added another teaspoon of oil and the wok is smoking hot now, I also added the noodles directly in the middle, what next?
Robot: Fry for a few more seconds, after that bring all of the vegetables from the sides onto the noodles and then stir-fry them together for about 1 more minute.
User: I have brought all of the vegetables from the sides onto the noodles and fried them together for another minute, what do I do next?
Robot: After this, you should add the curry paste and then stir-fry with the noodles and vegetables for another 20-30 seconds more.
User: I have stir-fried the vegetables for another 20-30 seconds more, what is the next step?
Robot: Now, divide the two warm bowls, then sprinkle over the chives or the spring onions and the red chili to serve.
