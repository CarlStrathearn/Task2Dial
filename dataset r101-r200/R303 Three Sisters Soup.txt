User: Today I would like to make Three Sisters Soup, what ingredients do I need to make this soup?
Robot: You will need ½ pound pinto beans, 1 tbsp of butter, 1 medium onion, 1 large garlic clove, 6 cups of low fat chicken broth, 1 large stalk of celery, 1 large carrot, 1 cup of yellow squash, 1 cup of tomatoes, 1 large potato, 1 cup of frozen corn kernels, 1 tbsp of tomato paste, 1 tsp of dried leaf oregano, ½ tsp of salt, ⅛ to ¼ tsp of black pepper, ⅛ tsp of dried red peppers and ½ cup of cilantro.
User:I have the ingredients ready to make the Soup, where do I begin?
Robot: Soak the dried beans overnight in cold water or boil them for a few minutes and let them stand for 60 minutes.
User I have dried the beans overnight in cold water and I let them stand for 60 minutes. What should I do next?
Robot: Now, rinse and drain the beans and add extra water to cover and then cook until done.
User: I have rinsed and drained the beans and added the extra water, what do I do next?
Robot: Good, now melt 1 tbsp of butter in a 4 and ½ quarter soup pot over a medium low heat, then stir in 1 medium onion and cook them for 2 minutes.
User: I melted the butter in a soup pot over medium heat and stirred in the onion, what do I do next?
Robot: Now add 1 large carrot, 1 cup of yellow squash, 1 cup of tomatoes and 1 large potato and simmer for an additional 30 minutes.
User: I have added the carrot, squash, the tomatoes and the potato and I have simmered them for an extra 30 minutes as required, what do I do next?
Robot: Stir in the undrained pinto beans, frozen corn kernels, tomato paste, 1 tsp of dried leaf oregano, ½ tsp of salt, ¼ tsp of black and red pepper flakes, then cook them for approximately 8 minutes until the corn is cooked and the soup is piping hot and finally serve with ½ cup of snipped cilantro, about 1 tbsp per bowl.
User: I have stirred the beans, corn kernels, tomato paste, oregano, salt and black pepper flakes and I have let them cook for 8 minutes, then I have served them with ½ cup of cilantro as required. What do I have to do next?
Robot: Now simply serve the soup in a bowl and enjoy.
