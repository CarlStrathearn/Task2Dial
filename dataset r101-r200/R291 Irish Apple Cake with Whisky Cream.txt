R291 Irish Apple Cake with Whisky Cream https://cookeatshare.com/recipes/irish-apple-cake-with-whisky-cream-44786

This recipe takes 45 minutes to cook, serves 10-12 and contains 16 ingredients,  [''butter'', ''sugar'', '’egg'', '’granny smith apples'', ''walnuts'', ''vanilla'’, ''baking powder'', ''soda'’, ''salt'’, ''cinnamon'’, ''nutmeg'’, ''flour'’, ''whipped cream'’, ''heavy cream'’, ''honey'’, ''Irish Whiskey'’]

User: I would like to bake an Irish Apple Cake with Whisky Cream, what are the ingredients required to bake this Apple cake?
Robot: To bake this cake you will need ¼ cup of butter, 1 cup of sugar, 1 beaten egg, 4 Granny Smith apples, ¼ cup of chopped walnuts, 1 tsp of vanilla, ½ tsp of baking powder, ½ tsp of soda, ½ tsp of salt, ½ tsp of cinnamon, ½ tsp of nutmeg, 1 cup of all-purpose flour, whipped cream, 2 cups of heavy cream, 2 tbsp of honey and 2 tbsp of Irish Whiskey.
User: I have all of the ingredients ready to bake the cake, what is the first step that I have to take?
Robot: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit, then grease an 8-inch square cake pan, then in a large bowl, cream ¼ cup of butter and 1 cup of sugar together until they become light and fluffy.
User: I preheated the oven to 350 degrees and greased the square cake pan as required, what do I have to do next?
Robot: Add 1 beaten egg, 4 Granny Smith apples, ¼ cup of chopped walnuts and 1 tsp of vanilla and stir well, then sift in the dry ingredients and mix them well.
User: I have added the eggs, apples, walnuts and the vanilla, I stirred and mixed them well, what do I do next?
Robot: Now, pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake until the cake is light brown and then place a skewer inside into the center and make sure that it comes out clean, for approximately 45 minutes.
User: I have poured the batter into the prepared pan and baked for 45 minutes as required, what do I have to do now?
Robot: Now, let the pan cool for 5 minutes, then unmold and serve hot or cold with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream, or the Whisky Whipped Cream.
User: I let it cool for 5 minutes and served hot, what next?
Robot: To make the whipped cream, start by chilling a clean stainless steel bowl, a whisk and the cream in the refrigerator or the freezer for 10 or 20 minutes, let them get nice and cold but not frozen.
User: I have chilled a stainless steel bowl, a whisk and the cream in the refrigerator, what do I do now?
Robot: Next, pour the cream after it has chilled inside the bowl and start to whisk, hold the whisk like a dagger with the wire part pointing down and use your wrist and elbow for motion.
User: I have poured the cream inside the bowl as required, what next?
Robot: Now, stick the whisk into the bowl at an angle and draw it through the cream in quick and sharp motions.
User: I stuck the whisk into the bowl at an angle and drew it through the cream, what do I have to do next?
Robot: Finally, serve the Whiskey Whipped Cream in a bowl next to the Apple Cake and enjoy the cake.
