User: I would like to make Small Clams today, what ingredients do I need to make them?
Robot: You will need 300g of fresh tulya, 1 brown onion, 2 shallots, 20mm knob fresh ginger, 1 stalk of lemongrass, 2 garlic cloves, 50g of butter, 500ml of light fish stock, 2 fresh chili leaves, salt and pepper to taste.
User: I have the ingredients ready, what is the first step?
Robot: Wash 300g of fresh tulya under cold water.
User: I have washed 300g of tulya under cold water, what do I do next?
Robot: Chop 1 brown onion, 2 shallots and 20mm of fresh ginger with a knife, then crush the garlic using the side of the knife.
User: I have chopped the brown onion, shallots and ginger, I also crushed the garlic, what do I do next?
Robot: Now, add the tulya and pour 500ml of fish stock together with cold water, then bring to a boil.
User: I added the tulya and poured the fish stock together with cold water, what is the next step after this?
Robot: Good, add 2 fresh chili leaves and cook over a high heat until the clams open up.
User: I have added the chili leaves and cooked over a high heat, what now?
Robot: Lower the heat, then season with salt, ground pepper and monosodium glutamate, then simmer over a lower heat for approximately 2 minutes.
User: I have seasoned with salt and pepper and also simmered over a lower heat for 2 minutes as required, what do I do next?
Robot: Good, now remove the curry leaves, then transfer to a soup bowl, serve the meal and enjoy.
