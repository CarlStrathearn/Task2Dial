User: I would like to cook Chicken and Coconut Curry, what are the ingredients required to cook this meal?
Robot: For the curry powder you will need a handful cooked basmati rice, 115g of coriander seeds, 30-40g of cinnamon pieces, 30g of black peppercorns, 30-55g of dried chili, 30g of cumin seeds and 85g of fennel seeds.
User: I have the ingredients, but what about ingredients for the curry?
Robot: You will need 2 tbsp of coconut oil or vegetable oil, a handful of fresh curry leaves, approximately 10-12 leaves, 1 finely sliced onion, 2 roughly chopped garlic cloves, a small piece of ginger peeled and roughly chopped, 8 chicken thighs, 2 tbsp of curry powder, 200ml of coconut milk, salt and fresh coriander to taste.
User: I have the ingredients ready to cook this meal, where do I begin?
Robot: First, for the curry powder, you must heat a heavy-based pan over a medium-high heat.
User: I have heated a heavy-based pan on a medium-high heat, what do I do next?
Robot: Now, once hot, add the basmati rice and let it cook for a couple of minutes.
User: I have added the basmati rice and I let it cook, what do I do next?
Robot: When the rice has started to brown, add 115g of coriander seeds, 30g of cinnamon pieces and 30g of black peppercorn pieces and let them toast for a couple of minutes when stirring.
User: I have added the coriander seeds, cinnamon and the peppercorn and I let them roast, what now?
Robot: Good, now add 55g of dried chili, 30g of cumin seeds and 85g of fennel seeds and then continue to roast them in a pan for another 5 minutes, until they are fragrant and toasted.
User: I have added the chili, cumin seeds and the fennel seeds, what next?
Robot: Now remove them from the heat and transfer to a large bowl and allow them to cool there.
User: I have removed them from the pan and transferred them to a large bowl, what do I do next?
Robot: Now once they are cool, grind together the spices in a spice grinder or pestle them in a mortar until a fine powder has been created.
User: I have grinded them together in a mortar and made a fine powder, what do I do next?
Robot: In order to make the curry, heat 2 tbsp of oil in a large pan and then add a handful of curry leaves, 1 onion, 2 garlic cloves and a small piece of ginger, then cook for approximately 10 minutes until the onions are soft.
User: I heated the oil in a large pan and added the curry leaves. I also cooked them for 10 minutes until the onions were soft. What next?
Robot: Now, add the chicken pieces to the pan and allow them to go brown, whilst frequently stirring.
User: I have added the chicken pieces to the pan, now what?
Robot: Now, stir 2 tbsp of curry powder and stir well to evenly coat all of the chicken pieces and continue to cook for another 10 minutes.
User: I have stirred the curry powder and the pieces are all coated, what do I do next?
Robot: Add 200ml of coconut milk and pour in 250ml of water, then sprinkle a teaspoon of salt and bring to a boil.
User: I have added the coconut milk, thank you and also poured the water in, what do I do now?
Robot: Now reduce the heat and cover it with a lid and allow the curry to simmer gently for approximately 30 minutes until the chicken is cooked through well.
User: I have reduced the heat and covered it with a lid and allowed the curry to simmer for 30 minutes, what do I do next?
Robot: Now, remove the lid and allow the sauce to reduce for another 5-10 minutes to thicken and stir occasionally.
User: I have removed the lid and stirred more, what do I do now?
Robot: Transfer to a serving dish and sprinkle with freshly chopped coriander to serve and enjoy the meal.
