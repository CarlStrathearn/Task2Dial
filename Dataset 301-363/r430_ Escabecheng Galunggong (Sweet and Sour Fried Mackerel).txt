User: I would like to make Escabecheng Galunggong (Sweet and Sour Fried Mackerel)
Robot: Descale, gut, and remove the gills of all 1kg of the fish. Fry them in a pan until they are golden brown before setting aside. 
User: I have prepared and fried the fish, what do I do next?
Robot: Next, sautee 5 cloves of garlic, minced in a garlic crusher. Roughly chop 1 onion and saute with the garlic until light brown.
User: I have sautéed the garlic and the onion. What next?
Robot: Next, add 180ml of water, 480g of pineapple chunks, and 2 carrots cut into sticks with a knife. Simmer these ingredients for 5 minutes. 
User: I have simmered these ingredients in the pan, now what?
Robot: Now, add 120ml of pineapple juice, 2 thinly sliced gingers, 15ml of maple syrup, 45g of flour and 1 tsp of salt. Stir these together well and simmer until the sauce becomes thick. 
User: I have added those ingredients and simmered into a thick liquid. What now?
Robot: Now cut 2 red bell peppers into strips using a knife, and add these to the pan, simmering for another 3 minutes. 
User: I have simmered the bell peppers in the pan for another 3 minutes. What is the next step?
Robot: Next, pour the sauce over the fish, serve and enjoy.
