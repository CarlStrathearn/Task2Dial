This recipe takes 10 minutes to prepare, 10 minutes to cook, serves 4 and contains 7 ingredients,  ['lamb chops', 'trader joe's 21 seasoning salute, 'water', 'garlic', 'salt’, 'oil', ‘vinegar’]

User: I would like to make pan seared lamb chops.
Robot: First clean the individually cut lamb chops by trimming the excess fat from the meat and the bone with scissors or a knife. In a bowl, soak the lamb chops in 30ml of vinegar with enough water to fully immerse the lamb chops. Soak them for 10 minutes.
User: The lamb chops have finished soaking. What now?
Robot: Now, drain the water and let the lamb chops sit in the bowl for another 5 minutes. After this, wash well with cold water.
User: I have rinsed and washed the lamb chops, what do I do next?
Robot: Next, pat the lamb chops dry and coat them with 30g of trader joe's 21 seasoning salute; 2 garlic cloves, grated with a garlic press; and 1 tsp of salt.
User: I have seasoned the lamb chops, what do I do now?
Robot: Now, splash some oil into a pan and sear each side of the lamb chops until cooked through. Serve with sides of your choice and enjoy.
