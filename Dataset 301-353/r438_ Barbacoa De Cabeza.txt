User: I would like to make Barbacoa De Cabeza
Robot: In a slow cooker combine all of the non-meat ingredients. Add 7g of garlic powder, 7g of black pepper, 15g of dried oregano, 7g of chilli powder, 7g of ground cumin, a sprinkle of salt, one bay leaf, and the juice from one lemon. 
User: I have added the ingredients to the slow cooker, what now?
Robot: Now, trim most of the fat off 1.1-1.5kg of cheek meat using scissors or a knife and place it into the slow cooker. Cook the ingredients on high for 5-6 hours or until the meat is tender. Top up with liquid if it begins to dry out.
User: The meat is tender, what do I do now?
Robot: Now, remove the meat from the cooker and chop into small pieces. Serve with tortillas and enjoy.
