User: I would like to make Tofu peanut vegan stir-fry
Robot: First, make the sauce. In a large bowl whisk 30ml of sesame oil, 45ml of tamari or soy sauce, 15ml of maple syrup, 15g of freshly grated root ginger, and 30ml of sesame oil along with some 100ml of water. 
User: I have whisked together all of those ingredients for the sauce, what now?
Robot: Now, cube 180 of drained tofu using a knife and cover it with half of the sauce, setting the rest aside. 
User: I have cut and covered the tofu with the sauce, what do I do next?
Robot: Next, put a wok over high heat, adding in 15ml of sesame oil. Fry the tofu for 5 minutes before adding 4 cloves of garlic, crushed with the side of a knife; a thumb sized piece of root ginger grated with the fine side of a grater; and 1 small red chilli, finely sliced with a knife. Fry this for a further 2 minutes. 
User: I have fried these ingredients together in the pan, what next?
Robot: Next, with a knife, deseed and slice lengthwise 1 red pepper and 1 yellow pepper. Chop the stems of 4-5 tenderstem broccoli and finely slice 5 spring onions with a knife and add all of these ingredients in the pan. Keep a handful to the side for garnish. Toss these together over a high heat for 3-4 minutes. 
User: I have tossed those vegetables over a high heat, what comes now?
Robot: Now add in 1 carrot, peeled into ribbons using a peeler. Add in 300g of straight to wok medium noodles and pour in the remaining sauce, seasoning with salt. Stir fry these ingredients until the noodles are coated in the sauce and heated through. 
User: I have stirred fried the noodles and added the sauce. What now?
Robot: Now serve with the leftover vegetables, 1 tsp of sesame seeds, and 30g of peanuts, chopped using a knife to garnish. Enjoy.
