This recipe takes 10 minutes to prepare, 10 minutes to cook, serves 4-6 and contains 7 ingredients,  ['fresh trout or salmon', 'flour', 'salt', 'pepper', 'vegetable oil’, 'soy sauce', 'lemon wedges’]

User: I would like to make Simple Pan fried trout. 
Robot: First clean and scale 4-6 trout or kokanee salmon. Remove the head if preferred to reduce the length in the pan. Sprinkle the fish with salt and pepper. 
User: I have prepared the fish, what now?
Robot: Now spread 240g of flour on a large plate and gently dredge each fish in the flour, turning to cover each side. Then, heat a large saute pan to a medium-high heat and put 15ml of vegetable oil in the pan. 
User: I floured the fish and heated a pan. What do I do next?
Robot: When the oil is hot place two fish in a pan, cover with a lid and cook for 2-3 minutes. Turn and cook on the other side for another 2-3 minutes. Remove from the pan and follow the same process for the remaining fish.
User: I have pan fried all of the fish, what now?
Robot: Next, remove the bones from the fish and fillet them. Serve with soy sauce and lemon wedges and enjoy.
