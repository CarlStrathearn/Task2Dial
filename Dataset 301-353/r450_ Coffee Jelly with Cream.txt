This recipe takes 5 minutes to prepare, 15 minutes to cook, serves 4-6 and contains 7 ingredients,  ['unflavored gelatin', 'water', 'hot water', 'sugar', 'nescafe classic’, ‘nestle cream’, 'brandy’]

User: I would like to make Coffee Jelly with Cream.
Robot: First soften 15g of soft gelatin in 60ml of water. Then add 480ml of hot water and 60g of sugar, cooking over a medium heat in a saucepan until the sugar and gelatin are dissolved. 
User: I have dissolved the gelatin and the sugar, what now?
Robot: Stir in 45g of Nescafe Classic instant coffee using a spoon and pour this mixture into 4 or 6 custard cups. Place these into a refrigerator and allow to set.
User: I have placed the mixture into the refrigerator, what next?
Robot: Next add 120g of sugar to a saucepan. Add in 120ml of water and cook the syrup for 2-3 minutes. Stir in 30ml of brandy before allowing it to cool down. 
User: I have made the brandied syrup, what do I do now?
Robot: Now serve the coffee gelatin with a teaspoonful of nestle cream and brandied syrup. Enjoy.
