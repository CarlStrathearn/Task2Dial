This recipe takes 5 minutes to prepare, 20 minutes to cook, serves 1-2 and contains 5 ingredients,  ['slab of salmon', 'bottle of furikake', 'yoshida sauce', 'wasabi', ‘mayonnaise’]

User: I would like to make Salmon Furikake. 
Robot: First, preheat the oven to 180ºC and generously sprinkle a bottle of furikake to cover salmon. 
User: I heated the oven and covered the salmon. What now?
Robot: Now bake this in the oven for 20 minutes. Once done, heat 350ml of yoshida sauce and pour it over the salmon. 
User: I baked the salmon and poured the yoshida sauce over the salmon. What do I do now?
Robot: Now mix some wasabi and some mayonnaise in a ziplock bag. Poke a hole in a corner and drizzle over the salmon. 
User: I have drizzled the salmon with the wasabi mayo, what now?
Robot: Now you’re ready to serve and enjoy.
