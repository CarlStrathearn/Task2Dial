This recipe takes 5 minutes to prepare, 10 minutes to cook, serves 3-4 and contains 9 ingredients,  ['squid', 'honey', 'soy sauce', 'vinegar', 'brown sugar’, 'garlic', 'ginger’,'hot chilli pepper', 'salt']

User: I would like to make Grilled Squid. 
Robot: First wash and clean 1kg of squid well, take out the black ink, the skin and film. 
User: I have prepared the squid, what now?
Robot: Now, in a bowl mix 120ml of honey, 120ml of soy sauce, 120ml of vinegar, 120g of brown sugar, half a head of garlic and 7g of ginger, minced in a garlic crusher, and 1 tsp of hot chilli pepper minced with a mortar and pestle. Add in 2 tsp of salt before mixing well together and adding the squid. Leave this to marinate for 60 minutes. 
User: I have made the marinade and placed the squid in it for an hour. What next?
Robot: Next, after the squid has marinaded, grill it for 4 minutes on each side.
User: I have grilled the squid, what is the next step?
Robot: The next step is to heat the remaining marinade in the microwave to use as sauce. Serve the squid with the heated marinade sauce and enjoy.
