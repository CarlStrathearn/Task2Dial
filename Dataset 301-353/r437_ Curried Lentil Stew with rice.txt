User: I would like to make Curried Lentil Stew.
Robot: First chop three slices of bacon with a knife. Fry them in a large pot over a modern heat until lightly browned. Chop one large onion using a knife and 150g of ham and add these to the pan, cooking for 5 minutes and stirring. 
User: I have cooked bacon, ham, and onion. What now?
Robot: Now, add a sprinkle of turmeric, cumin, ginger, and cloves to season the ingredients in the pan. Stir these together for a minute. 
User: I have stirred in the seasoning, what should I do next?
Robot: Next, add 480g of dried brown lentils to the pot. Using a knife, chop 2 large tomatoes, 3 medium carrots, adding to the pot with 1.6L of water. Sprinkle it with salt and pepper and bring to the boil, stirring well. Reduce to a simmer and cook these uncovered for 45 minutes until the lentils are tender. 
User: The lentils are tender, now what?
Robot: Now put a scoop of the pre-prepared boiled rice in each bowl and pour the stew over the rice and enjoy.
