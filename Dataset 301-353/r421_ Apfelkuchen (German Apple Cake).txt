User: I would like to make German Apple Cake
Robot: First set your oven to 180ºC and grease or spray a 23cm x 33cm baking dish and set it aside. 
User: I have heated the oven and prepared the baking dish, what do I do now?
Robot: Now, peel 3-4 apples with a peeler or a knife, and remove the core. Cut each apple into 6 segments.
User: The apples have been cut into segments, what next?
Robot: Next, in a bowl beat 180g of butter with 120-180g of sugar using a fork, cramping until it is light and fluffy. Then beat in 2 eggs and add a few drops of vanilla extract. 
User: I have beat those ingredients together and added the eggs, now what?
Robot: Now gradually add 420g of flour to the bowl as well as 2 tsp of baking powder and a dash of salt. Alternatively add in 180ml of milk in three parts, stirring these together carefully.
User: I have folded those ingredients together, what shall I do now?
Robot: Stir in 30g of raisins and spread the batter in a prepared baking dish. Arrange the apple wedges throughout the batter, pushing them down. Sprinkle another 30g of raisins over the top of this.
User: I have spread the batter and added the fruit. What now?
Robot: Now, bake in the oven for 25-35 minutes, or until golden brown. To serve, sift powdered sugar over the entire cake and enjoy.
